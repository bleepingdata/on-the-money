## Guidance belongs in skill files

The `.skills/` folder is the single source of truth for development
guidance — it is consumed by all developers and AI assistants (Claude,
Copilot, humans). When you learn something that should change how work
is done, update the relevant skill file. Do not rely on Claude memory
or conversation context to carry guidance that other contributors need.
